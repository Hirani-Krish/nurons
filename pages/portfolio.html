[ STATUS: DEPLOYED ] // ARCHIVE_02 // SECTOR: HEALTHCARE

Project: FORTRESS

Enterprise Data Lake Encryption & Zero Trust Migration

YEAR: 2024
DURATION: 6 MONTHS

Challenge: A healthcare data provider suffered a penetration breach on their staging environment, exposing a critical unpatched SQL injection vector. The client managed a 50TB patient data lake with strict HIPAA obligations and could not afford downtime during remediation.

Execution: Deployed a custom rotating-key KMS (Key Management Service) integrated with AWS HSM. Migrated all tables to a heavily indexed PostgreSQL cluster with field-level AES-256 encryption. Implemented strict Zero-Trust API routing via FastAPI with JWT + RBAC. Executed zero-downtime migration using blue-green deployment strategy.

Project: COUNSEL

AI-Powered Legal Document Analysis Chatbot

YEAR: 2025
DURATION: 5 MONTHS

Challenge: A boutique law firm needed an internal AI assistant capable of parsing, summarizing, and answering questions from large volumes of contracts, case files, and legal precedents — while maintaining strict data privacy and never sending documents to external APIs.

Execution: Deployed a fully local LLM (fine-tuned LLaMA 3) using RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) architecture backed by a Pinecone vector database. Project: NEXUS

Multi-Tenant Analytics & Reporting SaaS Platform

YEAR: 2025
DURATION: 8 MONTHS

Challenge: A B2B SaaS startup needed to build a scalable white-labelled analytics dashboard platform for enterprise clients to visualize their business KPIs. The system needed to handle hundreds of isolated client tenants without data bleed, while maintaining a sub-3 second dashboard load time even with complex aggregated queries.
